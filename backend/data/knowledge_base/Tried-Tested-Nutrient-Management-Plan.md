Introduction In this second edition of the Tried &Tested Nutrient Management Plan,the industry (AIC,FWAG,LEAF, NFU and CLA) has worked together to deliver an aid to making nutrient planning and recording simple and practical for you and your farm.
By using this plan you can manage your nutrients efficiently to save money and reduce environmental risks.The plan will also help you meet the latest NVZ regulations in a step-by-step,manageable way.
